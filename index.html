About Me


I am a final-year undergraduate student in Joint Honours Mathematics and Computer Science at McGill University. I am also an undergraduate researcher at Quebec AI Institute (Mila) and McGill NLP group, supervised by Prof. Jackie Chi Kit Cheung, and Rahul Aralikatte. Currently, my research is primarily focused on text generation and efficient pre-training. I am actively exploring other interesting topics. Previously, I participated in Google CS research mentorship program. For the academic year 2023-2024, I will serve as a Technical Project Manager at McGill Artificial Intelligence Society.
